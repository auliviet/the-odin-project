Camping recipes for 3 days - Option 1

Description

A selection of recipes for 3 days of camping in the woods. These recipes are designed to be simple, quick, use similar ingredients and only require limited equipment.

Breakfast

  1. Bacon & egg roll
  2. Pancakes
  3. Fruits & cereals

Lunch

  1. Vegetarian sandwich
  2. Greek salad
  3. Chicken caesar wrap

Dinner

  1. Gnocchi with pesto
  2. Haloumi burgers
  3. Fajitas with chicken

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • 8x wraps
  • 4x burger buns
  • Gnocchi

  • 1x tomato
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 1x Lebanese cucumber
  • Fruits
  • 2x red onions
  • 2x bags of leaves
  • 1x bag of lettuce
  • 2x capsicum
  • 1x avocado
  • 1x lime
  • Spinach

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 eggs
  • 8 slices of bacon

  • Pesto
  • Humus
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Caesar sauce

  • Jar of roast vegetables
  • Sundried tomatoes
  • Parmesan
  • 200g feta
  • Haloumi
  • Pancake mix

Breakfasts

Breakfast 1 - Bacon & egg roll

  1. Toast the burger buns on the pan. When ready add the barbecue sauce and set aside.
  2. Cook the bacon on the pan and add to the bread.
  3. Cook the egg on the pan abd add to the bread.

Breakfast 2 - Pancakes

  1. Add water to the pancake mix following the instructions. Shake.
  2. Place the pan on the stove to warm up.
  3. When the pan is hot, start cooking the pancakes.
  4. Dice fruits to add to the pancakes.

Breakfast 3 - Fruits & cereals

  1. Dice fruits and add to the cereals.
  2. Add milk or yoghurt.

Lunchs

Lunch 1 - Vegetarian sandwich

  1. Cut the breads in halves and spread pesto on one side.
  2. Add the roasted vegetables and sundried tomatoes.
  3. Shave the parmesan and add to the sandwich.

Lunch 2 - Greek salad

  1. Dice the cerry tomatoes, red onion and lebanese cucumber.
  2. Add the olives and leaves.
  3. Crumble the feta.
  4. Drizzle some balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Add salt and pepper.
  5. Mix all ingredients together.

Lunch 3 - Chicken caesar wrap

  1. Cook the chicken in the pan and cut into stripes.
  2. Add the chicken, sundried tomatoes and lettuce in a bowl. Mix in the caesar sauce.
  3. Place in a wrap.

Dinners

Gnocchi with pesto

  1. Place water to boil. Cook the gnocchi according to the instructions.
  2. Add the frozen spinach to a pan to warm. When warmed up, add the pesto sauce.
  3. Add the gnocchi to the spinach and pesto. Add the sundried tomatoes and shave parmesan on top

Haloumi burgers

  1. Toast the burger buns and add humus when toasted.
  2. Cook the haloumi in a pan and add to the buns.
  3. Slice the tomato and place on top.
  4. Place the salad on the side with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

Chicken fajitas

  1. Slice the red onion and capsicums.
  2. Caramelise the onion and capsicum in a pan.
  3. Place aside and add the chicken to the pan.
  4. When the chicken is cooked, cut into stripes and add to the capsicums and onions. Add some lime.
  5. Mash the avocado. Add salt and lime.
