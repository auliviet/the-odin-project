Camping recipes for 3 days - Option 2

Description

A selection of recipes for 3 days of camping in the woods. These recipes are designed to be simple, quick, use similar ingredients and only require limited equipment.

Breakfast

  1. Toasts
  2. Fruits
  3. Acai bowl

Lunch

  1. Falafel wrap
  2. Avocado toast
  3. Mexican salad

Dinner

  1. Quesadillas
  2. Burgers
  3. Hot dogs

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • 8x wraps
  • 4x burger buns
  • 4x hot dog buns

  • 3x tomatoes
  • 1x Lebanese cucumber
  • Fruits
  • 1x red onion
  • 2x brown onions
  • 2x capsicums
  • 2x avocados
  • 2x lime
  • 1x lemon
  • 1x can of corn
  • 1x can of black beans
  • 1x mango
  • 1x green chili

  • 500g mince beef
  • Falafels
  • 4x sausages

  • Tsatziki
  • Humus
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Tacos mix

  • Swiss cheese

Breakfasts

Breakfast 1 - Toasts

  1. Toast the bread on the pan. When ready add the barbecue sauce and set aside.
  2. Add peanut butter or Nutella.

Breakfast 2 - Fruits

  1. Cut fruits and place in a bowl.

Breakfast 3 - Acai bowl

  1. Dice fruits and add to the cereals.
  2. Add acai.

Lunchs

Lunch 1 - Falafel wrap

  1. Warm the falafels on the pan.
  2. Cut the cucumbers into strips.
  3. Add humus and tsatziki to the wrap. Add falafels and cucmbers.

Lunch 2 - Avocado toast

  1. Toast the bread on the pan.
  2. Mash the avocado.
  3. Add salt, pepper and lime and add to toast.
  4. Dice the mango and add on top of the avocado.

Lunch 3 - Mexican salad

  1. Dice the capsicums, 2x tomatos and 1x avocado.
  2. Drain the corn and black beans and add to the vegetables.
  3. Add salt and lime.

Dinners

Quesadillas

  1. Cook 250g of mince beef with the tacos mix.
  2. Place a tortilla, add cheese, the beef and the rest of the mexican salad.
  3. Fold in half and cook until the cheese is melted.

Burgers

  1. Form beef patties with the remaining 250g of mince beef.
  2. Toast the burger buns on the pan.
  3. Cook the patties on the pan and place on the buns.
  4. Slice the tomato and place on top.
  5. Add swiss cheese and barbecue sauce.

Hot dogs

  1. Caramelise the onions in the pan.
  2. Cook the sausages.
  3. Place in the hot dog buns and add barbecue sauce.
